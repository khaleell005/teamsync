The problem

Work gets lost in
the noise.

Tasks assigned over messages. Progress tracked in spreadsheets. Nobody knows what's overdue until it's too late. TeamSync fixes that.

📋
No single source of truth
Tasks scattered across chats, emails, and docs — impossible to track at a glance.
👁
Zero visibility
Managers can't see who's blocked, overloaded, or waiting on someone else.
🔀
Unclear ownership
When everyone owns a task, nobody does. TeamSync makes ownership explicit.
What you get

Built for teams that
move fast.

01
Admin Control
Create member accounts, set up projects, and assign tasks — all from one dashboard. You decide who sees what.
02
Personal Workspaces
Each member sees only their assigned projects and tasks. No clutter, no distraction — just the work that matters to them.
03
Real-Time Progress
Members update their task status and leave a progress note. Changes appear instantly for everyone on the team.
04
Color-Coded Team View
A shared board where every member's work is visible — each person identified by their own color.
05
Project Leads
Assign leads to oversee specific projects. They can reassign tasks and monitor progress without admin-level access.
06
Role-Based Access
Four roles — Admin, Project Lead, Member, Viewer. Each with exactly the right level of access, nothing more.
